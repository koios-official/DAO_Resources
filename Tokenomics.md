## Introduction

Koios, an innovative decentralized API platform anchored in the robust Cardano blockchain, introduces its native utility token - "Koios". This token is ingeniously crafted to streamline transactions within the Koios ecosystem, incentivizing nodes for their pivotal roles and facilitating users' access to a diverse range of data and services.

To catalyze the adoption of Koios, we have designed a strategic reward mechanism for both Full and Lite nodes. This mechanism is based on the volume of requests processed by each node, as meticulously tracked by the API Request Load Balancer statistics.

These vital statistics are gathered from the monitoring layer(s) and securely recorded on the Cardano blockchain at the conclusion of every epoch through transaction metadata submission. Subsequently, the rewards are calculated by proportionally dividing the predetermined epoch pot among the nodes that actively engaged in the Koios cluster during that epoch.

This approach to reward distribution serves a dual purpose: it motivates node operators to maintain active participation in the Koios cluster, and it fosters a competitive yet healthy ecosystem, crucial for the growth and stability of the decentralized infrastructure.

Recognizing the utility of the Koios token in facilitating payments for API services and as a reward for nodes, we outline the core principles of our tokenomics:

* **_Utility_**: The Koios token is a primary payment method within our API service ecosystem, ensuring fluid transactions and interactions.
* **_Incentive Mechanism_**: Nodes are rewarded with Koios tokens based on their service provision, promoting active participation and ensuring the infrastructure's robustness.
* **_Token Supply_**: A clear set of rules and mechanisms governs the total supply and minting process of Koios tokens, ensuring a transparent and predictable economy.
* **_Token Distribution_**: Strategic allocation of Koios tokens focuses on the ecosystem's growth and sustainability, supporting development, marketing, community incentives, and other key initiatives.
* **_Governance_**: Koios token holders may have governance rights, influencing the platform's direction and evolution.
  
## **Reward Calculation for Nodes**

In alignment with Koios' commitment to fostering a robust and participatory network, we have established a comprehensive token distribution strategy. 

This strategy is designed to incentivize and reward nodes that are integral to the Koios ecosystem. 

A key aspect of this approach is the allocation of revenue generated from the sale of advanced (non free) API access. Specifically, 90% of the tokens used to purchase Koios API advanced access will be redistributed to nodes participating in the Koios network. 

 This redistribution not only serves as a direct reward for their contributions but also ensures a continuous cycle of investment and growth within the ecosystem.
 
 Furthermore, to bolster the network's development, particularly in its formative years, the Koios treasury will contribute an additional 1000 Koios tokens to the node reward pool for each epoch. This contribution will be made for the first five years, significantly enhancing the incentive for nodes to participate actively. 
 
 The remaining 10% of the revenue from the sale of advanced API access will be directed to the Koios treasury. This allocation to the treasury is pivotal for the sustainable financial management and strategic growth of the platform.

**_Core Features of the Reward System_**:

**_Clarity_**: Koios employs a unique reward mechanism for Full and Lite nodes, incentivized by the volume of requests serviced. This is quantified through precise Load Balancer statistics.
 
**_Feasibility_**: The reward system is designed for scalability and adaptability, accommodating the network's growth without compromising efficiency.

 This strategy ensures that the nodes, as the backbone of the Koios ecosystem, are adequately compensated for their critical role in maintaining and expanding the network. By balancing immediate rewards with long-term growth incentives, Koios positions itself as a forward-thinking and sustainable platform in the decentralized API landscape.

## **Koios Token Distribution**: 

- **_Koios Core Developers (20%)_**: Recognizing the architects and maintainers of the project, ensuring their vested interest aligns with long-term success.
- **_Koios Contributors (10%)_**: Rewarding community contributions, fostering a sense of shared ownership.
- **_ISPO (30%)_**: Funding the project's growth, ensuring equitable distribution and engaging the community from inception.
- **_Treasury (30%)_**: A strategic reserve for governance, funding new developments, and ensuring resilience.
- **_Community Initiatives and Partnerships (5%)_**: Fueling collaborations and community-driven projects within the Cardano ecosystem.
**_Marketing and Future Development (5%)_**: Dedicated to expanding reach and evolving the platform.
